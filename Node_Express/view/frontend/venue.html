Philippine Arena

Spanning an expansive 99,000 square meters, the Philippine Arena stands as a premier venue equipped with cutting-edge technology. Its impressive architecture reflects the grandeur of the events it hosts, offering an unforgettable experience for attendees. Situated in Bulacan, north of Manila, this multi-purpose arena is designed to accommodate various large-scale gatherings. With a seating capacity of 55,000, it holds the distinction of being the Philippines’s largest indoor arena.

Araneta Coliseum

Araneta Coliseum

Covering a clear area of 15,400 square feet, the Araneta Coliseum is a historic and versatile venue located in Cubao, Quezon City. Known for being one of the oldest arenas, which dates back to 1960, it has hosted countless iconic events, from sporting competitions to concerts. With a total capacity of 16,500, the coliseum remains a top choice for large gatherings. Its strategic location offers convenient access via the LRT-2, making it easily reachable for attendees from different parts of Metro Manila.
